Featured Club: Robotics

Join our robotics club to learn programming and engineering skills!

What We Do

In the Robotics Club, students design, build, and program robots to complete challenges. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's something for everyone.

Why Join?

  • Learn coding and engineering skills
  • Compete in robotics competitions
  • Work as a team to solve problems

How to Join

The club meets every Wednesday after school in the computer lab. Speak to Mr. Smith in the IT department to sign up!
