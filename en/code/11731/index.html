Axolotl Care Guide

Learn how to take care of these fascinating amphibians.

What is an Axolotl?

Axolotls are aquatic salamanders native to Mexico. Unlike other amphibians, they remain in their larval stage for life.

How to Care for an Axolotl

Axolotls require clean, cool water and a diet of worms and pellets. They should not be handled often as their skin is delicate.

Setting Up the Right Tank

A good tank setup includes a filter, hiding spots, and a temperature of around 16-18°C.
